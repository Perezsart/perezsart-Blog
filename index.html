Inside my mind.

A safe space

Perezsart is more than a very common Spanish last name with the word ‘art’ attached to it, Perezsart is a safe space and a world I created for myself when I was 16 years old, a world where expressing emotions is not a cliché, where I can feel free to make mistakes and learn from it, perezsart is more than a simple painting or illustration, more than a poem or a lazy writing. This is me, I’m perezsart, I’m a safe space.

I created this blog with the intention to materialize that safe world I created, so that people like me or the people that feel related to my content could join the conversation.

Art is expression, art is passion and emotions, art can’t be tamed, art is revolution, art is a safe space for the ones that believe in it and receive it with open arms and open mind.

Welcome to Perezsart.
